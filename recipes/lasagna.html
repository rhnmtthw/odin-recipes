Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. Each layer is carefully assembled and baked to perfection, creating a hearty and comforting meal. The meat sauce, often made with ground beef or pork, is simmered with tomatoes, garlic, and herbs to develop a deep, savory flavor. The result is a dish that’s both satisfying and flavorful, perfect for gatherings or family dinners.

Over the years, lasagna has evolved into various regional and international versions. Some recipes include vegetables like spinach, zucchini, or mushrooms, while others use alternative proteins or cheeses. Vegetarian and even vegan lasagnas are popular too, showcasing how versatile the dish can be. Despite its variations, the essence of lasagna remains the same—warm, layered goodness that brings people together around the table.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or pork (or a mix of both)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1 egg (optional, for creamier texture)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Layers:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2–3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Optional Add-ins:

  • Spinach or mushrooms (for added vegetables)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

How to Cook Lasagna:

  1. Cook the meat – In a pan, cook chopped onion and garlic in a little oil. Add ground beef and cook until brown. Pour in tomato sauce, add salt and pepper, then simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  2. Boil the noodles – Cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Mix the cheese – In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese) with a bit of salt. You can also add a little Parmesan if you like.
  4. Layer the lasagna – In a baking dish, start layering:
    1. First, a bit of meat sauce
    2. Then noodles
    3. Then cheese
    4. Repeat the layers until full
    5. Finish with mozzarella cheese on top
  5. Bake – Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest and serve – Let it sit for 10 minutes before cutting. Enjoy!
